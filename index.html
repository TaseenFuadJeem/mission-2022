Global Community

Globalization refers to the rising global connections and interdependence that characterize modern life. Globalization is centered at the heart of culture and culture is at the heart of globalization. This means that the enormous changes of our time remain difficult to understand until they are grasped through the term ‘culture’. Culture harbors much weight in globalization because it is an intrinsic factor of sophisticated connectivity. Therefore, culture orientates people individually and as a whole towards particular actions which culminate to globalization.
